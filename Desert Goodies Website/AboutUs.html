Dessert Goodies Terengganu



Dessert Goodies Terengganu was incorporated in Malaysia
as a private limited company under the Companies Act, 1965 on 9 November 1983.
It is located at Kuala Terengganu,Malaysia. It was first establish on 18 October 2019.


Currently, there are 3 varieties of dessert which are freshly baked throughout the day
in each of this Hot-baked store. By using the best ingredients, like semi-sweet chocolate
chips and flavorful nuts, these homemade tasting dessert becamefamous just by word of mouth.
The "Free Smell" from our Hot-baked store has been an irresistible aroma of our freshly baked
dessert to be enjoyed by true dessert lovers everywhere.
